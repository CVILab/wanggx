Biography

I graduated from Chongqing University in June 2003 with a bachelor's degree in Information and Computational Science. In December 2019, I received a master's degree in Computer Application Technology from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. In February 2021, I graduated from Kunsan National University, Republic of Korea, majoring in Computer Information Engineering and obtained a doctor’s degree. I am currently a senior engineer and the director of the Institute of Computer Vision of Jiujiang University, a part-time researcher of Jiangxi Poyang Lake Ecological Science and Technology City, a member of the Computer Vision Committee, a reviewer, and an assistant editor of the Journal of Information and Communication Convergence Engineering.
